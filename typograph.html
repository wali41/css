Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and visually appealing. It’s more than just picking a cool font—it’s about how letters and words are placed to create mood, convey meaning, and guide the reader's eye. Whether it’s in a poster, website, or a book, good typography sets the tone before a single word is even read. From bold headlines that scream attention to elegant serifs that whisper sophistication, typography is the silent powerhouse of design.
